Curicullum Vitae

Georgios Methenitis was born in Amarousio Atikis in October 1988. He graduated from Technical University of Crete in Greece with a 5-year diploma in Electronic and Computer Engineering. In 2012 he completed his diploma thesis under the supervision of the assistant professor Dr. Michael Lagoudakis in the subject of multi-agent behavior and strategy coordination with the highest grade.

He received his Masters of Science degree after completing a two-years master studies at University of Amsterdam in the subject of artificial Intelligence.

From February 2015 he is a PhD. candidate at the intelligent systems group at centrum wiskunde and informatica in Amsterdam.
